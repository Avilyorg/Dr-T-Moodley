What is
Aviation Medicine?

Aviation medicine is a specialised field of healthcare focused on the physical and mental demands of flying. It addresses how altitude, reduced oxygen levels, acceleration forces, fatigue and environmental changes affect the human body, ensuring pilots and aviation personnel remain medically fit and safe to operate aircraft.
